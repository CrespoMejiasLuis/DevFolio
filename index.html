About Me

"Hello! I am a programming enthusiast studying a degree in video game creation and developement at the Universitat Jaume I. I am passionate about the world of video games in general, but I especially love strategy games, resource management games, and any subgenre of these.

Thanks to the university and my personal projects, I have been able to develop my other game developing skills, such as level design, narrative, art, and 3D modeling. I am excited to continue making games, and to keep growing both professionally and personally!"
